About the game

This is a simple game where you will need to find all the differences between the two pictures.

You can choose between two levels: Normal and Hard

The Normal level will give you simpler pictures where it will be easy to find mismatched items. A Hard level will give you more complex images with much finer details, and various animations will be installed in addition, which should make the situation even more difficult.

You will receive help in the upper right corner. Their number will vary depending on the level chosen. You can earn extra help by completing the picture without using any help.

Using macos or ios could lead to poor expierence. App works best with Chromium-based browsers on windows and android.
